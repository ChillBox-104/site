Crazy Biscuit

Type: FoodObtainability: BuyableRequires Rank: Yes

How to obtain:

The Crazy Biscuit can be bought from the Food NPC for 500 credits. It requires access to the "Premium Food" section.

Effects:

  • Heals you instantly
  • Feeds you instantly (gets you to 100% hunger)
  • Gives you the "Strenght 4" effect for 20 seconds
  • Gives you the "Regeneration 4" effect for 20 seconds
  • Gives you the "Speed 4" effect for 20 seconds
